Bellino is a well-crafted script typeface.
Drawn by Måns Grebäck during 2018, this high-quality font has beautiful letter shapes and swashy capitals.

Use one or more underscores after any word to underline.
Example: Bellino___

The font contains stylistic, initial and final alternates, as well as ligatures.
It also has extensive language support.
